HOW TO PLAY
  • Top 12 Dancers, 12 Singers and 6 Fashion talents have to register their ids with SK employees to participate in this event.
  • Pk Battle will be held on Republic Day.
  • dancers and singers’ talents must wear tricolor outfits and need to perform patriotic (deshbhakt) performances on this special Republic Day.
  • For fashion battle talents need to wear costumes according to Republic Day.
  • PK match will be held between the talents from same category, Dancer go against dancer, Singers go against singer, Fashion go against fashion.
  • Talents need to come live 15 min before the PK Battle, there will be a 15-min official PK match scheduled for the event.
  • First Talent to the left on the webpage schedule, will start the performance first and will perform for first 5 – 6 Min, then next talent will perform for 5 – 6 Min.
  • Will have 1 winner from each category (Dancing, Singing & Fashion) based on maximum beans received in the event will be mentioned in the BANNER for 3 days.
  • Talents and User will be ranked on the leaderboard based on the maximum beans received and spent respectively in the event.
  • Top Talent from each category has a chance to win a Spotlight slot.
  • Talent ranking will be separate for each category.
User receives 25% of total beans pot
